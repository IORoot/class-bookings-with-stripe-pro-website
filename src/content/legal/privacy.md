## What we collect

When you purchase Pro, Stripe processes your payment and provides us with your email address and payment metadata. We do not store card numbers.

When you use the download resend form, we process the email you submit to verify licence entitlement.

If you open a GitHub Issue, your report is subject to [GitHub’s privacy policy](https://docs.github.com/en/site-policy/privacy-policies/github-privacy-statement).

## How we use data

- Fulfil your purchase and send download links
- Verify licence status for download resend
- Respond to support requests

## Third-party services

| Service | Purpose |
|---------|---------|
| Stripe | Payment processing |
| Resend | Transactional email |
| Netlify | Hosting and serverless functions |
| GitHub | Plugin releases (private) and support issues (public) |

## Retention

Entitlement records are retained while your licence is active and for a reasonable period thereafter for support and accounting.
